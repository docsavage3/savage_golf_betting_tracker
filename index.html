üéØ Side Games Guide

Learn about all the exciting golf side games you can play with Savage Side Bets!

üéØ Murph

How to play: Call "Murph" before your shot on any hole. If you make it, you win. If you miss, you pay everyone.

Betting logic: Caller pays everyone if they miss, or gets paid by everyone if they make it. Risk vs reward!

Strategy tip: Best on shots you're confident about, but not too easy!

üèÜ Skins

How to play: Best score on each hole wins the skin. Ties carry over to the next hole, building up the prize!

Betting logic: Winner gets paid by all other players. Multiple skins stack up when holes tie.

Strategy tip: Play solid golf - consistency wins over risky shots.

‚õ≥ KP (Closest to Pin)

How to play: On par 3 holes, whoever gets closest to the pin wins. Must be on the green to qualify!

Betting logic: Closest player gets paid by everyone else. Simple and exciting on every par 3.

Strategy tip: Aim for the center of the green - being safe often wins!

üêç Snake

How to play: Get a snake (3-putt) and you owe everyone money. The snake "bites" you!

Betting logic: Anyone who 3-putts pays everyone else. Encourages good putting!

Strategy tip: Lag your first putt close - avoid those costly 3-putts!

üê∫ Wolf

How to play: One player is the "Wolf" each hole. They choose a partner OR go "Lone Wolf" for bigger stakes!

Betting logic: Wolf + Partner vs other team, OR Lone Wolf vs everyone else (3x payout!).

Strategy tip: Choose your battles wisely - going Lone Wolf is high risk, high reward!

üí° Pro Tips

  • Start Small: Begin with $1 bets to learn the games
  • Mix & Match: Play multiple games at once for more action
  • Stay Honest: All players should agree on rules before starting
  • Have Fun: These games make every shot matter!
