How to play: A player calls "Murph" before hitting their shot on a specific hole.

Betting logic: If they hole the ball within 2 shots, all other players pay them the bet amount. If they fail, they pay all other players the bet amount.

How to play: With 4 players: Teams compete on each hole. With 2-3 players: Individual players compete. The best score wins the skin(s).

Betting logic: Each skin is worth the bet amount. If no one wins a hole, skins carry over to the next hole. Winner gets paid by all other players.

How to play: Player who is closest to the pin on the Par 3 holes. Ball has to be on the green

Betting logic: The KP player gets paid the bet amount by all other players if they win the KP on that hole.

How to play: Snake tracks players who make a 3 putt.

Betting logic: Each snake increases the pot. The last player to get a snake owes the entire accumulated pot to the other 3 players.
